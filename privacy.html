Privacy Policy

We are commited to protecting your privacy. We will only collect and use your personal information in ways that are described in this privacy policy.


Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from you:

  • * Your name and contact information (such as your email address and phone number)
  • * Your payment information (such as your credit card number)
  • * Your demographic information (such as your age and gender)
  • * Your browsing activity on our website

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • * Provide you with the courses and services you have requested.
  • * Communicate with you about your account and your use of our services.
  • * Process your payments.
  • * Improve our website and services.
  • * Target advertising to you.

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • * Our third-party service providers who help us provide our services to you.
  • * Our marketing partners who help us target advertising to you.
  • * Law enforcement or other government agencies if required by law.

Your Rights

You have right to:

  • * Access your personal information
  • * Correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • * Request that we delete your personal information.
  • * Object to our use of your personal information.
  • * Restrict our use of your personal information.
  • * Port your personal information to another company.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@abclearningcenter.com.my

Security

We take steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. These steps include:

  • * Using secure servers.
  • * Encrypting your personal information when it is transmitted over the Internet.
  • * Requiring our employees to keep your personal information confidential.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or through a prominent notice on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@abclearningcenter.com.my
